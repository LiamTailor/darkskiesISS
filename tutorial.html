Dark Skies Tutorial

Hi! Thanks for collaborating in analyzing light pollution.

The application is really simple. It loads a photo from International Space Station and asks you to classify the photo:

  • Black
  • City
  • Stars
  • Aurora
  • Astronauts
  • None of these
  • or Broken image

Then, if the picture was taken at night, you will have to specify if the picture is showing a city, stars, or a different.

All you have to do is to click in one of the possible answers and it's done.
